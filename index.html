Before going any further, we ought to know more about parrots.

What are parrots?

According to Wikipedia, parrots (aka psittacines ) are birds made up of about 393 species commonly found in tropical and subtropical regiions.

They most distinctive features are their vivid and multi-colours, curved beak and upright stance.

However, their most interesting characteristic in my opinion is their intelligence. These birds are widely known for their ability to mimic humans as well as a panoply of sounds.
